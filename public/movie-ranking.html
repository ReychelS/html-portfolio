The Best Movies According to Reychel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The note book

This is my favorite movie. I love the beautiful love story. It is one of the fewest book adaptations that are better than the book itself

Me Before You (2009)

Beautiful and devastating at the same time.This unforgettable romantic tragedy introduces us to the witty Lou, who finally finds a job, this time as a caretaker.

Letters to Juliet (2010)

Amanda Seyfried is Sophie, a young girl on vacation in Italy who finds a “letter to Juliet”, one of many letters left in a fictional Verona’s courtyard. She finds out that there is a group of women called “secretaries of Juliet” who answer these letters and decides to join them and find out more about the relationships behind these words.
